About Us

The primary function of this tool is to expedite and assist observers who will be conducting observations using the MagAOX telescope. When an observer requests time to conduct observations, the telescope must be calibrated to their needs. Misinformation or mistakes in the calibration of the telescope can suck up vast amounts of precious time and money. As such, this tool seeks to mitigate, expedi te, and assist observations. This tool is designed to query SIMBAD and VIZIER, two astronomical databases, to collect information on the target stars, returning to the user the calibrations for the telescope — and other useful data for observing runs.
